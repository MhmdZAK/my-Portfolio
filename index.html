01 — About

Who I Am

I'm Mohammad Sakeer, a mechanical engineer with a passion for precision — in machines, in learning, and in life. My work spans the technical world of engineering and the deeply human world of coaching and mentorship.

I believe that growth is a system — one that can be designed, refined, and optimised. Whether I'm analysing stress loads or helping someone break through their ceiling, the principles are the same: clarity, discipline, and relentless improvement.

Beyond the blueprint and the whiteboard, you'll find me on the football pitch — where I study teamwork, resilience, and the beautiful geometry of the game.
